  • Welcome to All The Trivia! We’re a free trivia quiz website that’s forever under construction as we’re always adding new questions and more free trivia questions to make this site more valuable! However, our plan is to provide free trivia quizzes on an ever-expanding multitude of subjects. Do you have an idea for a specialized trivia quiz? Make sure to tell us via our contact form! This site provides a variety of trivia games for kids and adults. Topics covered include U.S. history, geography, Harry Potter, Disney films, science, sports, holidays (Christmas, Thanksgiving, and more!), general knowledge, Bible trivia, movies, music, you name it! All of our free trivia quizzes are in multiple choice format, so even if you don’t know the answer you’ve still got a chance to guess correctly!
